New features
- Drag-and-drop reordering of meal entries.
- Calendar streak indicator with tiered colors (white 1-6 days, blue 7-13 days, gold 14+ days).
- Daily goal progress dots on the calendar.
- Compact goals card now shows nutrient letters (P/G/C) and gram values inside the bars.
- Mini goals bar appears when scrolling past the goals card.
- Category icons for food items.
- OpenFoodFacts: Alt. DB button to retry failed searches on the alternative server.
- OpenFoodFacts: Faster barcode search — results appear immediately.

Changes
- Fork maintained by Alexeido (github.com/Alexeido/FoodYou).
- Meal entries now show only the primary brand name.
- Improved Spanish translations.
